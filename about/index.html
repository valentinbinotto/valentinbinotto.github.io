About

I am Valentin Binotto, also called “vale”, and I am interested in infosec and network tech.



Currently I work in the field of network engineering and plan to specialize in the area of linux and the use of open-source software for a freer internet. I am also interested in the analysis of malware and its threats to our security. Also cryptography and its mathematical basics belong to my interests.

I believe that the Internet can help people share their knowledge and make the world a better place. That’s why I’m a donor of the Electronic Frontier Foundation and a member of the Internet Society.


I like green tea, Japanese food and discussions related to politics and philosophy.
